BARR Neighbors Association

Residents living between Belmont, Ashland, Roscoe, and Racine.

Together, we are a stronger and safer community.

BARR Neighbors Association is a group of residents living between the boundaries of Belmont, Ashland, Roscoe, and Racine in the Lakeview neighborhood of Chicago.

We have a growing concern for the security of our area and are exploring options for contracting with private security firms to patrol the area. Our goal is to make our neighborhood a safer place to live, work, and play.

One of the largest hurdles to overcome is the question of how we will fund this project. We are looking for neighbors to pledge support for our project. We need your help to reach our goal!

Note: All residents within the boundaries will benefit from the private security regardless of their contribution.
